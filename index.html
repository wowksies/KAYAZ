Close to the metal

I'm kayaz — a self-taught developer who got pulled into the deep end of game internals and never left. Most of what I do lives a layer below the game: memory, drivers, and the cat-and-mouse of anti-cheat.

The Omnis brand is where that work ships. Omnis CS2 is fully developed and updated daily; Omnis R6 is what I'm building now. If it runs in ring-0 or talks to a game's memory, it's probably my idea of a good time.
